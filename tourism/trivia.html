Did you know that "Pandanggo sa Ilaw" is originated from Lubang?


PANDANGGO SA ILAW
Pandanggo sa Ilaw is a very popular folkdance in the Philippines. It is said to have originated in Lubang. Since Pandanggo sa Ilaw is a native dance from Lubang , the Office of the Sangguniang Bayan of the Municipality of Lubang passed an ordinance that mandates all barangays of the Municipality to participate in Pandanggo s Ilaw Festival every 12th day of August with street parade and presentation to promote camaraderie and local tourism. All schools are required to actively participate on this festive event to showcase their student's talent and nationalism.
