PRIVATE CAR:

🚗 If you're coming from Manila, the faster route would be via Tagaytay-Nasugbu then you can park your vehicle near the Nasugbu port complex or have your vehicle onboard the ferry but could be very expensive.

COMMUTE:

🚍 From Manila, you can head to the ff. terminals & bus companies bound for NASUGBU:

 —MRT Taft: BSC and San Agustin

 —Buendia: DLTB

 —PITX: San Agustin (direct to the port)

First trip leaves around 03:00-04:00AM

Travel time: 3 hours; Fare: ~Php160/head

🚕 From Nasugbu proper, hire a tricycle to Wawa Port. If you have no supplies yet, you may request the driver to bring you to the Market to have your last minute shop.

Travel time: 10 minutes;  Fare: ~Php80/trip (good upto 4pax)

🚢 In Wawa Port, head to the ticketing booth. Buy a ticket going to Tilik Port of Lubang Island. There's only ONE ferry scheduled daily that leaves the port between 09:00AM — 10:00AM.

Shipping Fare

Travel time: 3 hours; Fare: Php400 (economy) Php450 (aircon)

🚍At the Port of Tilik in Lubang Island, depending on where you are going to stay, you can ride a tricycle, van or jeep. It is mandatory for every tourist / traveler to drop at the Tourism Office in Lubang Proper so you can arrange your guide and tours.

Travel Time: 30 minutes, Fare: P100/trip
